Mate'Morphosis

  About Mate Morphosis  

Mate Morphosis is a chess-inspired puzzle game built around the concept of becoming the piece you capture. Outsmart each board, adapt to evolving mechanics, and hunt down the rival king across every stage.

 Features

  • Pixel-art graphics with retro charm
  • Increasing difficulty levels
  • Immersive sound effects and music
  • Extra gameplay mechanics beyond classic chess

Controls

  • Mouse
  • Gamepad / Touch

 Roadmap

  • Basic progression system
  • More levels & challenges
  • Extra mechanics to expand strategy
  • Accessibility options (colorblind mode, etc.)
  • Potential mobile / web version testing
