About Us

The story behind Wise Monke Entertainment Studio and our passion for game development.

Our Mission

Wise Monke Entertainment Studio is an indie game development studio passionate about creating unique experiences that challenge conventions and spark imagination. We believe in the power of innovation and gameplay

Our Philosophy

We craft games that we want to play, they should be fun and innovative. From satirical stealth adventures to mind-bending puzzle games, we're here to entertain, provoke thought, and push the boundaries of indie gaming. Every pixel tells a story, and every mechanic serves a purpose.

What We Do

We focus on creating memorable experiences that stay with players long after they've put down the controller.
